Multidisciplinary Vision and Passion for Innovation

My journey is driven by the passion to connect different areas of knowledge, creating innovative solutions and fostering continuous learning. I believe in the power of interdisciplinarity to train complete professionals ready for the challenges of the future.

With a solid foundation in engineering and information technology, I have dedicated my career to integrating disciplines and applying innovative methodologies in teaching and project development. In the last five years, I have had the privilege of teaching over 40 distinct subjects across approximately 30 undergraduate courses, with the primary goal of preparing professionals for the real challenges of the market.

My teaching experience extends to supervising 53 interdisciplinary projects, 16 final year projects (TCCs), and 1 scientific initiation project, which resulted in the publication of several works in institutional congress proceedings and journals. In 2025, I had the honor of supervising final year projects awarded as "Best TCC" in Biomedical Engineering, Computer Engineering, and Production Engineering at Centro Universitário FAM, with projects ranging from portable multiparameter monitoring to industrial safety analysis and consumption forecasting in production lines. My journey reflects a commitment to overcoming disciplinary barriers, encouraging the practical application of knowledge, and training professionals capable of facing the complexities of the contemporary world.
